## 🎯 Detailed Feature Walkthrough

### AI Legal Assistant
**Location**: Dashboard → Quick Access → Ask AI Assistant
**Features**:
- Type your legal question
- Get structured responses with:
  - Problem explanation
  - Suggested steps
  - Legal actions available
  - Relevant laws
- **Anonymous Mode**: Toggle to ask without login
- Disclaimer: This is guidance, not legal advice

**Example Questions**:
- "I bought a defective product"
- "My employer didn't pay my salary"
- "Someone is harassing me online"
- "I have a property dispute"

---

### FIR Guidance
**Location**: Dashboard → Quick Access → FIR Guidance
**Steps**:
1. **Select Issue Type** - Choose from 8 categories:
   - Harassment/Abuse
   - Fraud/Scam
   - Theft/Robbery
   - Cyber Crime
   - Domestic Violence
   - Property Dispute
   - Employment Issue
   - Defamation

2. **Fill Details** - Enter:
   - Location of incident
   - Detailed description

3. **Preview & Download** - Get:
   - FIR draft
   - Steps to file at police station
   - Required documents checklist
   - Your legal rights
   - Next steps after FIR

**Pro Tip**: Keep the FIR number for future reference

---

### Case Outcome Simulator
**Location**: Dashboard → Quick Access → Case Outcome Simulator
**How it Works**:
1. Select your case type (6 options)
2. Choose severity level (Low to Critical)
3. Rate your evidence strength (Weak to Very Strong)
4. Get prediction showing:
   - **Likely Outcome** - What probably happens
   - **Success Probability** - % chance of winning
   - **Time Estimate** - How many months it takes
   - **Legal Path** - Which forum/court
   - **Recommendations** - What to do next

**Important**: This is an estimate based on similar cases. Always consult a lawyer.

---

### Case Tracker
**Location**: Dashboard → Quick Access → Track Cases
**Features**:
- **Add New Case** - Click "Add Case" button
- **Track Multiple Cases** - Keep all cases organized
- **Monitor Dates** - See days until next hearing
  - Automatic urgency alerts for hearings within 7 days
- **Update Status** - Choose from:
  - Filed
  - Hearing Scheduled
  - Pending Decision
  - Appealed
- **Store Notes** - Add custom notes per case
- **Edit/Delete** - Manage your cases
- **Demo Data** - 2 example cases included

**What to Track**:
- Case number
- Case title
- Current status
- Next hearing date
- Court/forum name
- Important notes
